# Europe's First Underwater Museum Opens in Spain
**The Wall Street Journal - World News:** [January 25, 2017](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=MzjwxnxStSo)
*  Some 200 life-size human sculptures submerged 46 feet under the Atlantic Ocean. [[00:00:00](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=MzjwxnxStSo&t=0.0s)]
*  They form part of Europe's first underwater museum, [[00:00:09](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=MzjwxnxStSo&t=9.0s)]
*  which has officially opened to the public on the ocean bed off the Canary Island of Lanzarote in Spain. [[00:00:13](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=MzjwxnxStSo&t=13.0s)]
*  Funded by the local government, the Atlantic Museum is accessible to scuba divers and snorkelers. [[00:00:20](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=MzjwxnxStSo&t=20.0s)]
*  The figures, seen here being put into position, were created by British artist Jason de Kair Taylor. [[00:00:27](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=MzjwxnxStSo&t=27.0s)]
*  They're made from environmentally friendly and pH-neutral materials [[00:00:35](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=MzjwxnxStSo&t=35.0s)]
*  and will attract plant and animal life, just like artificial reefs. [[00:00:39](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=MzjwxnxStSo&t=39.0s)]
*  According to a statement released by the Atlantic Museum of Lanzarote, [[00:00:44](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=MzjwxnxStSo&t=44.0s)]
*  the first part of the project, installed in February 2016, [[00:00:49](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=MzjwxnxStSo&t=49.0s)]
*  is already frequented by angel sharks, barracuda and sardines. [[00:00:53](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=MzjwxnxStSo&t=53.0s)]
*  The artworks also include a 100-tonne, nearly 100-foot long wall and a botanic garden. [[00:00:58](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=MzjwxnxStSo&t=58.0s)]
*  The exhibition also features an installation called The Raft of Lampedusa, [[00:01:06](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=MzjwxnxStSo&t=66.0s)]
*  a sculpted boat carrying 13 refugees. [[00:01:12](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=MzjwxnxStSo&t=72.0s)]
*  It pays tribute to those who died during the recent migrant crisis. [[00:01:16](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=MzjwxnxStSo&t=76.0s)]
*  De Kair Taylor's other underwater exhibitions are located in Mexico, the Bahamas and Granada. [[00:01:21](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=MzjwxnxStSo&t=81.0s)]
